Past Projects

Areia Games

My first role was as a Game Designer on Xmen - Battle of the Atom, a mobile card battle game.

X-Men: Battle of the Atom

Players collected cards for various Xmen and their related villains and used them in a stat based PvE and PvP environment.

Play Next (Formerly Aeria Games)

When development began on a new project that would become Nova Smash, I took on the role of System Designer.

Nova Smash

Nova Smash was a pinball inspired physics game. Players collected heroes with special abilities which functioned as the "ball" during play.

Kabam

I was hired as a Content Designer by Kabam to work on Star Wars: Uprising, an Action RPG.

Star Wars: Uprising

Players progressed through a story and earned new gear to unlock abilitiy sets. It utilized touch input for movment and attack.

Rumble Entertainment

At Rumble, I worked as the Lead Character Designer (mechanics, not aesthetics) on Alliance: Heroes of the Spire.

Alliance: Heroes of the Spire

Players collect heroes, gear them up, and use their abilities to problem solve PvE and PvP encounters

Cloudcade

At Cloudcade, I started as the only designer on a new title called Card Brawl, but I also worked as a Product Manager for the same title.

Card Brawl

What started as a PvP only lane based tactics game morphed into a PvE only roguelike with dynamic level generation.

Cloudcade

While at Cloudcade I also pitched in on feature design and spec writing for their other title Shop Heroes.

Shop Heroes

Imagine if you took the crafting and auction house from World of Warcraft and made it its own game. That is Shop Heroes.
